Three copy directions based on the strategic brief, brand positioning, and Diaz's Reddit-onboarding reference. Each option covers the same 4 steps but with a different tone and framing.

|  | Option | Tone | Approach |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |

  **OPTION A: YOUR MONEY, UNLOCKED**

*Tone: Bold & Action-Oriented*

**HERO SECTION**

**Headline:** Your money. Unlocked.

**Subhead:** You just opened more than a payout method. You opened a bank account. Here is how to make it work for you in the next 14 days.

**CTA Button:** Let's go

**STEP 01: ADD YOUR CARD TO YOUR WALLET**

**Hook:** Start spending today. Not next week.

**Body copy:** Your physical card is on its way, but you don't have to wait. Add your virtual Crimson Visa Debit Card to Apple Wallet, Google Wallet, or Samsung Wallet right now and start tapping to pay immediately.

**How to do it:** Dasher App \> Earnings \> DoorDash Crimson \> View card details \> Add to Wallet

**Value prop:** Every tap-to-pay purchase with your Crimson Card earns you cash back on gas at the pump and EV charging. Your first fill-up could be your first reward.

**STEP 02: CHECK OUT YOUR ACCOUNT NUMBER**

**Hook:** This isn't a prepaid card. This is a real bank account.

**Body copy:** Go to your Crimson Hub and look at your Account Details. You'll see a real account number and routing number. That means direct deposits, bill payments, transfers in and out. All yours.

**How to do it:** Dasher App \> Earnings \> DoorDash Crimson \> Crimson Hub \> Account Details

**Value prop:** Your routing number is 075972202\. That's Starion Bank, Member FDIC. Your money is federally insured, just like any major bank.

**STEP 03: LINK YOUR OTHER BANK**

**Hook:** Move money in. Move money out. No drama.

**Body copy:** Connect an external bank account so you can transfer funds whenever you need to. No overdraft fees. No minimum balance. No surprises.

**How to do it:** Open your Crimson Hub and follow the steps to link an external account.

**Value prop:** Instant transfers when you need them ($1.99). Free standard transfers in 3-5 business days. Plus fee-free withdrawals at 40,000+ MoneyPass ATMs.

**STEP 04: SET UP A DIRECT DEPOSIT**

**Hook:** All your money. One place. Up to 2 days early.

**Body copy:** Use your Crimson account and routing number to direct deposit from your other gigs, your 9-to-5, or anywhere else you earn. Over 70% of Dashers juggle multiple income streams. Now you can manage them all from one account.

**How to do it:** Grab your account and routing number from Crimson Hub \> Enter them at your employer's payroll portal

**Value prop:** With Early Payday, you could access your paychecks up to 2 days before everyone else.\*

**PERKS SECTION**

**Headline:** 

\+ Cash back on gas at the pump

\+ 2% cash back on EV charging

\+ Exclusive Shell gallon discounts

\+ Savings on auto maintenance through Jiffy Lube & Valvoline

**FAQ SECTION**

**Section title:** Wait, I thought Crimson was...

**Q: Isn't Crimson just the Red Card?**

A: No. The Red Card is a prepaid card you use to pay for Shop & Deliver orders. Crimson is your personal bank account with a Visa debit card, instant earnings, and cash back.

**Q: What happened to DasherDirect?**

A: DasherDirect was the old program. Crimson replaced it with better features, real banking, and more perks. DasherDirect was sunset in April 2025\.

**Q: Can I still use Fast Pay?**

A: Once you activate Crimson, you won't need to. Your earnings hit your account instantly after every dash, with zero fees. No more paying $1.99 to access your own money.

**COMPLIANCE FOOTER**

\*Early Payday: Access to direct deposit funds up to 2 days early is not guaranteed and depends on when the payer submits the deposit. DoorDash Crimson Deposit Account is established by Starion Bank, Member FDIC. The DoorDash Crimson Visa Debit Card is issued by Starion Bank pursuant to a license from Visa U.S.A. Inc.
