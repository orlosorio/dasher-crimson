Your 4-step setup

Set it up now.
Thank yourself later.

1
Step one

Start spending today. Not next week.

Your physical card is on its way, but you don't have to wait. Add your virtual Crimson Visa Debit Card to Apple Wallet, Google Wallet, or Samsung Wallet right now and start tapping to pay immediately.

How to do it
Dasher App › Earnings › DoorDash Crimson › View card details › Add to Wallet
Why it matters

Every tap-to-pay purchase with your Crimson Card earns you cash back on gas at the pump and EV charging. Your first fill-up could be your first reward.

2
Step two

This isn't a prepaid card. This is a real bank account.

Go to your Crimson Hub and look at your Account Details. You'll see a real account number and routing number. That means direct deposits, bill payments, transfers in and out. All yours.

How to do it
Dasher App › Earnings › DoorDash Crimson › Crimson Hub › Account Details
Why it matters

Your routing number is 075972202. That's Starion Bank, Member FDIC. Your money is federally insured, just like any major bank.

3
Step three

Move money in. Move money out. No drama.

Connect an external bank account so you can transfer funds whenever you need to. No overdraft fees. No minimum balance. No surprises.

How to do it
Crimson Hub › Link an external account
Why it matters

Instant transfers when you need them ($1.99). Free standard transfers in 3–5 business days. Plus fee-free withdrawals at 40,000+ MoneyPass ATMs.

4
Step four

All your money. One place. Up to 2 days early.

Use your Crimson account and routing number to direct deposit from your other gigs, your 9-to-5, or anywhere else you earn. Over 70% of Dashers juggle multiple income streams. Now you can manage them all from one account.

How to do it
Crimson Hub › Copy account + routing number › Enter at payroll portal
Why it matters

With Early Payday, you could access your paychecks up to 2 days before everyone else.*
